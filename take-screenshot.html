Take a Screenshot of Pixel Knight Adventure

This page will help you take a screenshot of your game for the README.md file.

  1. The game is loaded in the iframe below.
  2. Play the game to get to a good-looking scene.
  3. Press the "Take Screenshot" button.
  4. The screenshot will be downloaded as "screenshot.png".
  5. Move the screenshot.png file to your project folder.
